West Afircan Chicken Stew

Ingredients

  • 4 uncooked chicken legs
  • 2 tbsp coconut oil, ghee, lard, or tallow
  • 1 large yellow onion chopped
  • 1 tsp fresh ginger minced
  • 3 large garlic cloves minced
  • 1 lb red bell peppers seeded and chopped
  • 1/2 cup of bullion stock or bovril
  • 1 lb sweet potatoes peeled and cut into chuknks
  • 1 1/2 cups chicken stock
  • 2 cups crushed tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup almond butter
  • 1/2 tsp ground coriander
  • 1/2 tsp ground cinanamon
  • 1/2 tsp ground cumin
  • 1/2 tsp sea salt
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tbsp cilantro chopped

Directions

  1. Salt the chicken pieces, heat the fat in large soup bowl.
  2. Brown the chicken, may need to be done in batches.
  3. Set chicken pieces aside as they brown.
  4. Saute onions in fat for 3-4 minutes.
  5. Add ginger and garlic for another 1-2 minutes.
  6. Add sweet potato and bell peppers.
  7. Add browned chicken, chicken stock, tomato paste, almond butter and spices.
  8. Bring to simmer for 5-7 minutes.
  9. Then cover pot and simmer on low for 90 minutes.
  10. Stir in cilantro, then serve.
