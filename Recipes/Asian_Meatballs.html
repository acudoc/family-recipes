Asian Meatballs

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds groundbeef
  • 1 medium shallot minced
  • 2 tbsp minced cilantro
  • 8 medium fresh shiitake mushrooms minced
  • 1 1/2 tbsp paleo friendly fish sauce
  • 2 tbsp coconut oil melted
  • 1/2 cup of bullion stock or bovril
  • 2 tbsp tomato past
  • 3/4 cup sweet potatoe minced
  • pinch sea salt
  • pinch fresh ground pepper

Directions

  1. Line baking sheet with foil and preheat oven to 375
  2. Combine mushrooms, shallot, sweet potato, and cilanto in large bowl.
  3. Add beef, fish sauce, and tomato paste.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Thoroughly combine the ingredients, do not over work the meat.
  6. Brush coconut oil on foil-lined baking sheet.
  7. with hands roll out three dozen meatballs around baking sheet.
  8. Bake for 15-20 minutes, rotating the tray at the midpoint.
  9. Serve with rice or rice noodles.
